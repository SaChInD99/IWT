Beach weddings

Considering a wedding at a beach that isn't close to home? A destination wedding is a fun way to get married while you, your family and friends are all on vacation together. Unlike a hometown affair, you can't assume everyone will be able to attend, especially if the journey is far and costly.... Having your wedding on the beach? For shore-loving couples, there's nothing more romantic than the sounds of the surf, the sand between your toes and the sultry, salt-tinged air as you say your vows. And if you want to invite the whole gang, think about a fun-filled wedding weekend and reunion at an activity-rich resort, beach or lakeside town.
